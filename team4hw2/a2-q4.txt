stdio.writeln(a)
writes a as is, a float.
stdio.writeln(a + 1.0)
Add 1.0 to the a float.
stdio.writeln(8 // int(a))
Divides 8 by a converted to integer, also omits the decimals. The result is integer.
stdio.writeln(8.0 // a)
Divides 8 by a, also omits the decimals. The result is float.
stdio.writeln(int(8.0 / a))
Divides 8 by a, but converts it to integer, omitting decimals.
